Information Page

Humans as a whole are leaving a negative impact on the enviroment.
If we continue at the pace we are currently at who knows how long
it will be until there is no enviroment left for us!

STATISTICS:

  1. The world has already lost 80% of its forests
  2. 27% of our coral reefs have been destroyed
  3. We are using up 50% more natural resources than the Earth can provide
  4. 10-20 million Tons of plastic ends up in our oceans every year

HOW TO HELP:

  1. Use reusable bottles instead of plastic bottles
  2. Recyle whenever possible
  3. Find creative ways to reuse items instead of throwing them out
  4. Turn off lights when you leave your house
