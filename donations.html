Environmental Defense Fund

The Environmental Defense Fund is an organization which is working to find solutions to major problem such as climate change, oceans, wildlife, health, etc. They work governments, communities and businesses across the world to educate people on the importance of environmental health and how to identify emerging issues. The EDF advocates for positive environmental actions and influences policies for environmental change. Click here to learn more:

The Nature Conservancy

The Nature Conservancy is an organozations that works to protect the lands and water around the globe. They are mainly focusd on finding solutions to climate change, fire, fresh water, forests, etc. Together with millions of members and scientists, they hope to create a world where humans and nature thrive.
